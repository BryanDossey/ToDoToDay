ToDo ToDay Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

ToDo ToDay respects your privacy.

ToDo ToDay stores tasks and application settings locally on your device using on-device storage.

ToDo ToDay does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Require an account
  • Track user activity
  • Use analytics services
  • Display advertisements
  • Share data with third parties
  • Upload your data to external servers

All task information remains on your device and under your control.

If future versions of ToDo ToDay introduce cloud synchronization or additional services, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
